FAQ

Why Computer Science?

Computer Science provided me with a pathway to utilize my creativity to solve problems that matter. I see technology at the forefront of the future and I see myself being part of this movement.

Which is my favorite programming langauge?

I feel most comfortable in object oriented programming languages like Java and C+.

What is my background in Computer Science?

My journey as a Computer Scientist began in 2021 as a college freshman. I had a generic understanding of where I was heading, but I have come to appreciate the extent of Computer Science through various course work.

What is my greatest quality?

My greatest quality as a Computer Scientist has been my ability to troubleshoot effectively. I have great attention to detail and am able to debug errors well.

Am I involved in any extracurricular activites?

I have had my greatest involvment at church where I am the Sound Engineer. I have the respobsibility of controlling sound, music, and live transmissions. I am also actively involved in Society of Hispanic Porfessional Engineers (SHPE) where I had the opportunity to attend the SHPE National Convention in Salt Lake City.

What types of employers am I looking for?

I am looking for a company that shares my values to make a positive impact on the world. I want to work for an employer that will provide me with mentorship to help others and make my job meaningful.

How has my experience as a First-Generation student been?

Navigating my path in Computer Science presented unique challenges, particularly due to the absence of mentorship. As a first-generation student and the STEM major in my family, the journey was consistently challenging. Despite facing these difficulties and the impact they had on my confidence, they didn't deter my progress. I've come to realize that my journey offers a distinctive perspective, and I believe that overcoming significant hardships ultimately molds us into exceptional professionals in our field.
